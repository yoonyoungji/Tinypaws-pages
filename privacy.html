1. Information TinyPaws stores locally

TinyPaws stores a small set of app preferences on your Mac so the app can restore your settings the next time you open it.

  • Selected character
  • Selected size
  • Selected opacity
  • Selected walk speed

These preferences are stored locally on your device using standard macOS app storage and are not sent to us.

2. Permissions

TinyPaws may request or guide you to macOS Accessibility and Input Monitoring settings so the desktop character can react to keyboard input while other apps are active.

Permission checks are used only to determine whether keyboard-reactive features can work. TinyPaws does not use these permissions to build a user profile, track browsing, or send your keystrokes to a server.

3. Diagnostic logs

TinyPaws may write simple diagnostic messages to a local temporary log file on your Mac for app status and permission troubleshooting.

These logs stay on your device unless you choose to share them with support.

4. Data TinyPaws does not collect

  • No account registration information
  • No payment information
  • No analytics profile tied to your identity
  • No cloud sync data
  • No browsing history collection
  • No personal data transmission to the developer as part of normal app use

5. Third-party services

TinyPaws does not require an online account or a hosted backend service to provide its core desktop companion features.

6. Your choices

  • You can decline Accessibility or Input Monitoring permissions.
  • You can remove the app at any time.
  • You can contact support if you have questions about how the app works.

7. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if TinyPaws adds new features or data practices. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
